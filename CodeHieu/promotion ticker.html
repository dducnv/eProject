PROMOTION ticket price for STUDENTS nationwide - Only 100K / ticket !!

News | 28/12/2019

From January 1 to June 10, 2020, Sun World Danang Wonders officially launched a shocking promotion for all students studying at universities, colleges and intermediate schools nationwide. Accordingly, the entrance fee to Sun World Danang Wonders is reduced to only 100,000 VND / package ticket. Young people only need to present their student card when purchasing tickets at the entrance to the park.

This offer gives students the opportunity to experience the world's 22 most modern outdoor games system such as: Sun Wheel - Top 10 largest spins in the world, Singapore Sling - top 10 thrilling games most in the world, Vietnam's tallest free-falling tower Golden Sky Tower, Vietnam's largest hang glider Queen Cobra ... And more especially, young people have the opportunity to experience new things that Highway games Boats debuted on January 1, 2021 bring.

Here, the traveling devotees also take a trip on the Monorail train, explore miniature Asia and admire the typical cultural architecture of 10 countries. After having fun, young people can relax in the cool green campus, enjoy the culinary quintessence of 3 typical regions with hundreds of delicious dishes.

Student ticket discounts are one of many incentive programs launched by Sun World Danang Wonders on the occasion of the new year, to help young people have more opportunities to experience regional entertainment at coastal city of Da Nang.
