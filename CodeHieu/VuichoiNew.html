[SHARE] TOP 5 extremely unique nightlife places for Da Nang!

News | 16/1/2021

Da Nang is not only famous for its poetic and romantic beauty in the daytime, but at night, this city is also gradually changing to become more and more attractive and romantic. So what to play in Danang when the night comes? This article will help you explain that question with 5+ activities and nightlife places that cannot be missed in Da Nang!

Da Nang is increasingly transforming, more attractive and poetic with sleepless nights

1. See Da Nang at night at night at Asia Park - Asia Park

Asia Park is an ideal destination for visitors not only during the day but also at night. This place is both the gathering place for entertainment activities, shopping and also the most diverse and unique cultural culinary space in Da Thanh.

Asia Park is an ideal tourist destination for tourists when coming to Da Nang

To visit Da Nang, but especially to go out at night, Asia Park is probably the most ideal choice. Asia Park with the highlight of the Sun Wheel - Sun Wheel is 115m high and more than 20 games, including 7 thrilling games and stylish photo angles, Asia Park is an attractive entertainment spot. very large with domestic and foreign tourists.

Asia Park - Asia Park with the highlight of the giant wheel of the Sun Wheel

At night, when the shimmering lights are lit on the sleepless city , what could be better if you can sit with friends and relatives in the giant wheel of the Sun Wheel and watch the dreamy city? That will be your memorable experience during your journey to discover Danang!
Here are some information about the impressive Sun Wheel - Sun Wheel that you may not know:

  • Altitude: 115m
  • Number of cabins: 64
  • Capacity: 6 people / cabin
  • Artistic illumination with 13,000 LEDs mounted on 32 spokes of the wheel
  • Recording time: 15 minutes / time

2. Cruise experience on Han River

When you plan not to sleep with Da Nang city at night, you should not miss the cruise experience on the Han River. The feeling of sitting on the yacht and watching the charming scenery of Da Nang with a little breeze in her hair is really romantic and peaceful.

Cruise on the Han River is an enjoyable experience in Danang at night

Infomation:

  • Ticket price: about 150,000 VND / person; Children from 1m to 1.3m charge 70% of adult fare; Children under 1m are free.
  • Ticket price includes exploring beautiful landscapes on both sides of the Han River, exchanging live music on a cruise, enjoying Cham Pa dance (not including food).
  • The pick up point is the marina at 32 Bach Dang.
  • Enjoying time is about 45 minutes / trip, there are 4 flights per day.
  • Served from 18h30 - 21h45 daily.

3. Fire hard at Sky36 - the highest Sky bar in Vietnam

Sky36 is a great destination for those who love the vibrant bustle of passionate dances at night in Da Nang city. Sky36 has an altitude of 166m, is the highest sky bar in Vietnam with a very classy and luxurious architectural space. This is also an interesting destination for those who want to have fun in Da Nang at night.

The vibrant space of Sky36 at night

4. Watch the Dragon Bridge spray fire, spray water on weekends

It will be a pity if you come to have fun in Da Nang at night and skip the watching Dragon Bridge spraying fire and water every weekend. At 21:00 every Saturday and Sunday, the Dragon on the bridge will spray fire and water, creating a very unique and extremely interesting scene. If you get to see this scene with your loved ones, you will be the happiest person!

Dragon Bridge spits fire and water in Da Nang every weekend

Above are 4 activities and entertainment places Da Nang at night that any visitor should try once. Hope this article will help you have more useful information to make your journey to discover Da Nang more interesting. Quickly schedule and enjoy the fun!
