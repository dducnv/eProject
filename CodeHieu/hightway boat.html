Sun World Danang Wonders "unveiled" the super product Highway Boat!

News | 27/12/2019

From January 1, 2012, Sun World Danang Wonders officially "unveiled" the flagship Highway Boat hang glider. The new game brings a lot of excitement, making the park dubbed "Asia miniature" a must-visit destination for tourists when coming to Da Nang.

Considered one of the most popular hit games this year, the Highway Boat hang glider will take you up and down along winding curves that promise to create a very interesting feeling. . With its 180-degree aerial twists, Highway Boat gives visitors the feeling of freedom in the air. The roller coaster will move quickly through each bend at a speed of 30km / h and balance the state comfortably and gently when preparing to the finish.

"Father" of Highway Boat is the world-famous game maker Intamin Amusement Rides (Switzerland) with over 50 years of experience and 47 global awards. Before officially welcoming the first guests to experience the thrill of juggling with this new game, Highway Boat has been tested and downloaded by leading experts of Intamin company on extremely strict standards. tight to ensure maximum safety for players.
In the top of the most attractive game genres in the world today, Highway Boat will surely make visitors filled with excitement and fun in the first days of the new year.

Especially, since January 1, 2019, Sun World Danang Wonders has launched a series of attractive incentive programs for customers:

  • Discount for students is only 100,000 VND / ticket.
  • Offer Da Nang people with special price from 100,000 VND / ticket, children (from 1m-1m4) 80,000 VND / ticket, under 1m free.
