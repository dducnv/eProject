Largest hang glider in Vietnam at Sun World Danang Wonders!

News | 01/1/2020

Vietnam's leading park Sun World Danang Wonders (under the Sun World entertainment brand system, Sun Group) in Da Nang launches a new game Queen Cobra - Vietnam's largest hang glider. Queen Cobra offers a thrilling experience that satisfies adventure enthusiasts when coming to the park.

Established as the largest hang glider in Vietnam with a track length of up to 689m and with 20 seats, Queen Cobra is on the list of the world's top adventure games, inspired by the image of a Snake. king cobra is over 5 meters long in Kerala India.
As a unique product of manufacturer Vekoma (Netherlands), a world-famous brand specializing in the production of adventure games is present in famous parks such as Walt Disney's amusement park system (Disneyland). , Universal Studios, Queen Cobra are finely designed to bring guests up to a height of 34m, free plunge at a speed of 80km / h, with 5 continuous 360 turns. Within 96 seconds, the twisting rails will give visitors an unprecedented excitement. Queen Cobra promises to bring explosive moments, satisfying adventurous followers who want to discover and conquer themselves.

Queen Cobra is the third Vekoma-branded roller coaster that Sun World Danang Wonders owns, next to Port of Sky Treasure and Garuda Valley. However, this is the most modern game , and it is also Asia Park's biggest game so far. Queen Cobra also raised the total number of outdoor games at Sun World Danang Wonders to 20. Queen Cobra brings endless fun moments for visitors. Not only experiencing the record hang glider, Sun World Danang Wonders will "treat" four-way customers with free the entire Softplay Zone, Sunblaster air gun shooting zone and 64 indoor slot machines. The world's leading modern games will give you endless summer fun experiences, like the Monorail monorail that takes you on a journey through 10 exciting Asian countries, the Singapore Sling - top 10 The most thrilling game in the world, Vietnam's tallest free-falling tower Golden Sky Tower, Angry Motor fun racing track ... The green space with 5,000 newly planted trees of Sun World Danang Wonders this summer will help Game devotees enjoy having fun, not afraid of the heat.

Putting more games into operation is the effort of Sun World Danang Wonders in constantly innovating, offering visitors new discoveries and many different experiences at the cultural park of the region. Along with the system of parks and amusement parks throughout Vietnam, Sun Group is making efforts to contribute to the construction and development of infrastructure for the tourism industry - a smoke-free industry in Vietnam.
