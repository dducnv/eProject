NOTICE: PREVENTION OF VIRUS CORONA

News | 03/8/2020

DEAR CUSTOMER!

Before the outbreak of the new Corona virus, Sun World Danang Wonders has taken the following measures to prevent the disease:

1. PUT DRY EXCELLENT HAND WASHING AT THE SERVICE DISTRICT
2. CONVENTIONAL CLEANING, CLEANING, AND FUMIGATION IS PERFORMED AT THE STATION / ELEVATOR / CABIN HOUSE AND OTHER PUBLIC AREAS.

At the same time, Sun World Danang Wonders would like to update customers with measures to reduce the risk of getting a new Corona virus strain as recommended by the Ministry of Health:

HAND WASHING FREQUENTLY WITH ROOM CLEAN OR CLEANER:
  • 1. Scrub thoroughly inside and outside of hands for at least 20 seconds.
  • 2. Use clean, disposable paper to dry hands or dry with a hand dryer in hot mode, avoid using towels and clothes to clean hands.
  • 3. Wash hands after coughing or sneezing.
USE THE CORRECTLY EXPORT
  • 1. Make sure the mask covers your mouth and nose.
  • 2. Avoid touching the mask while in use, only hold the strap when removing the mask.
  • 3. Use only once and then throw it in a safe, covered trash can.
  • 4. Wash your hands after removing the mask.
FIND TO MEDICAL FACILITIES
  • 1. If you have symptoms of fever, cough or difficulty breathing, DO immediately notify the medical staff of Sun World Danang Wonders at hotline: 0911305568 or call the hotline 19003228 of the Ministry of Health to provide information and advice. knowledge about disease situation nationwide and receive counseling on disease prevention.
  • 2. Share your travel schedule with medical staff.
IMPORTANT NOTE
  • 1. Do not spit indiscriminately in public.
  • 2. Avoid close contact with wild or domestic animals, live or dead.
  • 3. Use only foods that are cooked and safe to use.
  • 4. Keep body warm, drink plenty of water, rinse your mouth with antiseptic water.
LEARN MORE ABOUT VIRUS CORONA
  • 1. Corona is a virus that causes respiratory infections to acute pneumonia with symptoms such as high fever, cough, difficulty breathing, wheezing, ...
  • 2. Corona virus spreads from person to person.
