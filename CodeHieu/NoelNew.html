Sun World Danang Wonders turns “SWEET COUNTRY” in Christmas season!

News | 20/12/2020

From December 22 to December 26, a colorful European Christmas festival will turn Sun World Danang Wonders into a "Kingdom of sweets" with full of white snow, and a giant sweet pine tree that makes many visitors. must be excited, admired.

This Christmas season, With 50,000 VND / ticket, visitors to Sun World Danang Wonders will be amazed at a colorful scene of sweets, sparkling, fanciful in the colors of decorative lights and The scene is filled with white snow. White snow rains, giant candy pine trees, funny reindeer, lovely take visitors on an adventure, discovering European Christmas.

Christmas tree is made from 500 plastic bottles

The ambiance is bustling throughout the park, as visitors can enjoy many performing arts activities at the gate area such as Flashmob performance with the lively and cheerful old lady and Santa Claus, or the sweet candy Canival.

Another Christmas season is coming, the New Year is also approaching. Come to Sun World Danang Wonders to experience the special new aftertaste that the park offers visitors, as a miraculous Christmas gift instead of gratitude.
