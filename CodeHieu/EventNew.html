From December 20, 2020: Asia Park has returned spectacularly with a series of events to celebrate the new year 2021 with countless special offers.

News | 18/12/2020

Welcome back from December 20, 2020, Asia Park - Asia Park of Sun Group will accompany Da Nang city in the program "Da Nang welcomes the new year 2021", providing a vibrant festive season. with a series of monumental art and cultural events. In addition, there are great deals - free entrance to the entrance with food combo - super attractive fun.
After a long period of stopping to welcome guests due to disease and renovation, Asia Park will return spectacularly at Christmas (from December 24, 2020), with an attractive new appearance and an unprecedented series of special events.

In particular, Asia Park will accompany the Han River city in the program "Da Nang welcomes the new year 2021", marking the strong return of Da Nang after translation, with a series of special events and activities. The city is held right at the park, creating unique highlights in the tourist season to welcome Christmas and New Year 2021.
Specifically, the grand opening music night for the program "New Year's Da Nang 2021" will take place on December 30, 2020 at the Sun Wheel stage square of Asia Park, with the participation of of top entertainment stars such as singer Duc Phuc, singer / songwriter Le Cat Trong Ly, Bach Cong Khanh, Radio band, HIT International dance group from Sun World Ba Na Hills, Fly dance group, DJ Tenshi… The top performances on the sparkling stage will bring visitors uplifting moments in the emotional end of the year.
Not only one night of reuniting with the stars, the explosive return of Asia Park - Asia Park also brings visitors to Da Nang for the year-end a "holiday with idol stars". A series of art shows will be held every night from December 30, 2020 to January 2, 2021, from 19:30 to 21:30, Sun Wheel Square - Asia Park - Asia Park, with the participation of young stars such as Phuong Anh Idol, Khong Tu Quynh ... and many other famous artists.

Adding more jubilant joy to visitors to the park on this occasion, Asia Park also welcomes the audience with performances of the traditional Dragon Lion and Flower Plum on the breathtaking water stage. These performances are considered a very unique "specialty" of Asia Park, which will be performed at the water stage at 17:00 and 19:00 daily, from December 20, 2020 to February 2. / 1/2021 (except December 28, 2020).
A Noodle Festival held for the first time in the tourist capital of Central Vietnam will also take place in the Sun Wheel Square - Asia Park - Asia Park, from December 29, 2020 to January 2, 2021, in the time frame of 15:00 - 22:00 daily. 50 stalls serving the whole world of noodle dishes, from Quang noodle, noodle soup, pho, vermicelli, banh canh ... all over the three regions of Vietnam to the noodle dishes that have created the typical "flavor" of the country. such as Thai mixed vermicelli, Italian spaghetti, Chinese wonton noodles, Korean udon noodles ..., the Noodle Festival offers visitors a whole journey to explore the lands through famous noodle dishes. In particular, within the framework of the Noodle Festival, housewives and babies will surely enjoy trying one day as a "culinary artist", hand-coated noodles, making cakes in Quang's style. The contest "Mi Quang standard" took place on December 31, 2020.
Coming to Da Nang in the last days of the year, if you do not have the opportunity to roam the Korean market or visit many points to buy specialties, visitors can "buy Da Nang" right at Asia Park. A lot of specialties of the Central capital such as dried seafood, sesame dry cakes, tea, spring rolls, fish sauce, dried fruit ... will be displayed and sold right at Sun Wheel Square - Asia Park - Asia Park, from December 29, 2020 to January 2, 2021.

Jubilantly welcome guests back to Da Nang, visit and have fun at the park, a series of incentives will also be applied by Asia Park. Specifically, in addition to free admission tickets, many extremely attractive unlimited dining combos will cost only VND269,000 / adult / weekday, and VND 319,000 / adult / weekend. The combo ticket includes a range of delicious buffet at Buffet World Restaurant and free all games (except for kids tram, water games and other car rental services).
Striving with the city to organize a series of spectacular events, promising to bring the most exciting moments to visitors in the busy year-end days, the return of Asia Park - Asia Park not only creates should be a particularly impressive highlight for visitors when choosing to Da Nang to welcome the new year 2021. Moreover, new marks after a long time of stopping to welcome visitors of a park for many years are familiar destinations of Local people and visitors to Da Nang will also bring many very interesting surprises for those who come back to Asia Park for this special occasion.
