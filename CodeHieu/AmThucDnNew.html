"360 Degree Danang Cuisine" with more than 10+ places to eat in Da Nang!

News | 16/1/2021

Referring to Da Nang, many people have to admire because the beauty is so poetic and worth living in this coastal city With the following 10+ places to eat snacks in Da Nang, you will see why the culinary culture of Da Nang's snacks also contributes to making this place a paradise as seen. . If you once set foot in this land, do not miss these delicious restaurants!

Danang is a paradise of rich and unique cuisine

1. Asia Park Night Market - Asia Park

The snack stalls in the Asia Park night market are the place where visitors should go if they want to enjoy delicious snacks at night. The aroma of the barbecue here is really attractive and seductive.

Anyone who has visited this place should stop by to enjoy the variety of cuisines here because the dishes are varied and delicious at affordable prices. Snacks from East to West, from Vietnam to Korea, ... all include: spring rolls, Da Lat grilled rice paper, kimbap, tteokbokki, mixed rice, meat skewers, pancakes, Quang noodles, ...
Address: No. 1 Phan Dang Luu, Hoa Cuong Bac, Hai Chau, City. Danang
Operation time: 15:00 - 22:00 on weekdays
Average price: 10,000 VND - 50,000 VND / Person
The snack stalls at Asia Park - Asia Park are always full of people passing by because of the charm of the dishes there.
In addition, when coming to Asia Park - Asia Park (Indian area), visitors should not miss Ha Long Coconut Pearl Milk Shop.
It is a wonderful location amidst the magnificent magnificent space of the Indian White Castle on the banks of the romantic Han River. The shop's pearl yogurt dishes are delicious and refreshing. It is definitely a great beverage for visitors to visit this place.
Ha Long coconut pearl yogurt at Asia Park - Asia Park (India area)

2. Ms. Duong's pancake

This is one of the famous snack restaurants in Da Nang because of the deliciousness of the pancakes combined with the rich sauce that brings its very own secret. In addition, Ms. Duong restaurant also has other snacks such as: grilled pork vermicelli, grilled beef, spring rolls for customers to have more choices. Ms. Duong's restaurant is always crowded and is crowded in the morning and late afternoon.

Address: K280 / 23, Hoang Dieu, Hai Chau, City. Danang
Operation time: 9:00 - 21:00 on weekdays
Average price : 20,000 VND - 55,000 VND / person
Pancakes make up the brand of Banh xeo Ms. Duong

3. Ms. Thuy's snack shop

Ms. Thuy's snack shop is famous for her snacks such as: grilled combo, butter rice paper, milk tea, cheese stick, mixed rice paper, french fries, ... Her restaurant is not very large but always very crowded. guests and mostly young people.

Address : 68/2 Le Dinh Ly, Vinh Trung, Thanh Khe, City. Danang
Operating time : 7:00 - 22:00 on weekdays
Average price : 10,000 VND - 20,000 VND / person
Ms. Thuy's shop is always crowded with affordable snacks

4. Co Hang mixed rice paper - PCT mixed rice paper

This is a very popular snack bar that attracts students and students from Da Nang. The sour and spicy taste of the rice paper in combination with quail eggs and dried beef ... has created a very familiar and delicious taste, making the name and brand of the restaurant.

Address: Near the intersection of Le Loi, Hai Chau, City. Da Nang (next to Phan Chu Trinh school)
Operation time: 9:00 - 20:00 on weekdays
Average price: 7,000 VND - 20,000 VND / person
The rice paper cake attracts customers of Ms. Hang

5. Aunt Hoa sandwiches

Like Phan Chu Trinh mixed rice paper shop, Aunt Hoa's rice paper is very delicious and the price is very cheap. My aunt's shop is very crowded and mainly students. The favorite dishes of the students are: pate rice paper, mango fish sauce, wet egg omelet cake, fried rice paper, ...

Address: 62 / 2A Nui Thanh, Hai Chau, City. Danang
Opening time: 14h30 - 21h30 on weekdays
Average price: 5,000 VND - 12,000 VND / person
Aunt Hoa's rice paper rolls are very delicious and attract customers, especially young people

6. Hue cake pressed Khanh Khanh

Hue cake from Khanh Khanh is one of the very famous snacks in Da Nang. The restaurant has a lot of options for diners such as fried filter cake, roasted pork powder tea, rice paper mixed with tamarind sauce, egg pressed cake, ...

The famous Hue juice cake in Da Nang

7. Thanh snail shop

For those who love the sweet taste of snail dishes, you should come to Thanh. Here, you will have the opportunity to enjoy many delicious dishes such as snail mixed vegetables, snails, snails, jackfruit, ... Snail sauce here is all delicious: both rich and delicious. flavor substances Da Nang.

Address: 22 Hoang Hoa Tham, Thanh Khe, City. Danang
Working time: 16h30 - 00:00 on weekdays
Average price: 15,000 VND - 50,000 VND / person
Thanh snail restaurant is a very attractive place for young people who love snail dishes

8. Co Van butter cream

Co Van buttercream is the most famous butter cream shop in Bac My An market. It is nearly 30 years old and is a familiar destination for avocado ice cream lovers and visitors when coming here.
Creamy butter with cream and soft cream with dried coconut and milk ... combined to create a very attractive and bold butter cream cup. Make sure anyone who eats it once will forever remember that sweet and delicious taste!

Address: Bac My An Market, Ngu Hanh Son, City. Danang
Operation time: 7:00 - 18:00 on weekdays
Average price: 8,000 VND - 10,000 VND / person
Co Van buttercream has a delicious taste and is also very cheap

9. Aunt Hue's shop

Aunt Hue restaurant is famous for its dishes such as: banh canh, mixed rolls, fried flour, ... The restaurant is very crowded in the afternoon, so you should come early to eat your favorite dishes, avoid being out of stock.

Address: K75 / 15 Hung Vuong, Hai Chau, City. Danang
Working time: 15h30 - 20:00 on weekdays
Average price: 15,000 VND - 20,000 VND / person
Banh canh is a specialty of Aunt Hue with its very delicious taste

10. Ba Co fried sour spring rolls

There are many choices for you when going to Ba Co snack shop such as: baked goods, guava, plum tea, ... and especially fried spring rolls. Fried spring roll is also a dish that makes the restaurant famous for its delicious taste.

Address: 59/5 Nguyen Tri Phuong, Thanh Khe, City. Danang
Working time: 14h30 - 22:00 on weekdays
Average price: 7,000 VND - 35,000 VND / person
Fried spring roll is a specialty dish of Ba Co restaurant

Above is a synthesis of TOP 10 famous and famous Danang snacking places. If you have once set foot here, do not hesitate to try it out, will you?
