Park Character

 With many famous cartoon characters dressed up by
the staff and will make the children more excited,
not only that, the characters taking pictures with
you will make the picture more fun.

Some typical characters:
 - Hello Kitty:

As one of the famous cartoon characters associated
with the childhood of many children, at Da Nang
Kitty park will be able to participate in fun
dancing activities with the dance team to make
them more happy.
