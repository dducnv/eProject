Program "End of year price" - ONLY 50.000 VND

News | 10/12/2020

From now to the end of December 31, the largest cultural and entertainment park in the Central region of Sun World Danang Wonders will launch the promotion program "End of year shock price". Accordingly, visitors to the park freely experience hundreds of games and thousands of attractions, only for 50,000 VND / ticket.
The above rates are only applicable to Vietnamese tourists who are adults and children from 1m tall and above, children under 1m are free of charge. Especially, the preferential price is applied for all days of the week, including weekends and special holidays such as Halloween, Christmas ...

With a ticket price of only 50,000 VND, visitors can experience all the games available at Sun World Danang Wonders, except for Carnival with prizes. Park opening hours from 15:00 to 22:00 daily.
For a long time, Sun World Danang Wonders has a reputation as an ideal entertainment spot in the heart of Da Nang for groups of friends and families. Modern game systems in the top of the continent such as Singapore Sling, Golden Sky Tower, Queen Cobra, Highway Boat, Port of Sky Treasure, Love Lock, Monorail and most recently, Paradise Fall, Sun World Danang Wonders brings a world of endless discoveries to young people who love speed.

After moments of ultimate pleasure with games, a rich culinary system in the park, from Asian-style restaurants such as Panda, Indo Express, to cool outdoor fast food stalls shady trees Trees will treat the whole family with unforgettable delicacies, with extremely reasonable prices.
Of course, if you do not like adventure, you do not want to play kids, you can completely enjoy Sun World Danang Wonders your way, by climbing Monorail to go around sightseeing, or by walking around. 10 Asian countries, through iconic architectural works such as City Gate, Clock Tower, Sun Wheel, Dragon Boat, Buddha Statue ... or simulation works of Angkor Wat ...

Everlasting fun, delightful play, jubilantly with festivals, art shows prepared on a grand scale from now to the end of the year, families and groups of friends can completely schedule fun. week at Sun World Danang Wonders, to make the most of this unprecedented "year-end shock price" offer.
