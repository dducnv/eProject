To Da Nang to celebrate the new year of 2021 with a series of special attractive events at Asia Park - Asia Park

News | 28/12/2020

A series of epic events at Asia Park

"Da Nang - Welcome New Year 2021" is a series of large-scale events organized by Da Nang city taking place from December 29, 2020 to January 2, 2021. The focus of the series of events is a series of rich entertainment, arts and culinary activities at Sun Group's Asia Park - the companion unit in Da Nang city in this festival.

The opening of the festival will be the grand music night on December 30, 2020, at the Sun Wheel stage square of Asia Park, with the participation of top entertainment stars such as German singers. Phuc, singer / musician Le Cat Trong Ly, Bach Cong Khanh, Radio band, HIT International dance group from Sun World Ba Na Hills, Fly dance, DJ Tenshi ... The concert night promises to bring a vibrant space for attendees to have a really impressive experience before the new year.

Not only one night of December 30, 2020, within the framework of the festival "Da Nang welcomes the new year 2021", the audience will be reunited every night with idol stars such as Phuong Anh Idol, Khong Tu Quynh ... and other artists. Other famous artists, through art shows, street music are held continuously from December 30, 2020 to January 2, 2021, in the time frame from 19:30 to 21:30 at Sun Wheel Square - Asia Park - Asia Park.
