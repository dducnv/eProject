"Have fun" with Membership card of Sun World Danang Wonders!

News | 1/1/2019

From January 1, 2019, Asia Park will launch the product package "Membership Card" with an unprecedented incentive, only 500,000 VND / year of drop-off experience at Sun World Danang Wonders for all visitors.

With this powerful card, visitors can enjoy unlimited free play all the top games in the world such as Golden Sky Tower, Singapore Sling, Sky Treasure, Love Locks, The Flying Kirins ... Along with the iconic art architecture of 10 Asian countries and diverse culinary chains. This is also one of the many preferential programs offered by Sun World Danang Wonders on the occasion of the new year, with the desire that all visitors have more opportunities to experience the world-class entertainment space. in the coastal city of Da Nang.
