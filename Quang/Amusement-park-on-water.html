Amusement park on water

- Description: From June 1, 2019, Sun World Danang
Wonders officially put into operation an amusement
park on the water, with many relaxing games such
as duck riding, boat walking on the lake to give
visitors the experience. Completely new experience.
Visitors will be able to drive a duck boat around
the lake while watching, chatting and checking in.
This will be a relaxing, romantic experience for
young people, couples or small families every
afternoon. - Ticket price: 50,000 VND
/ game / 30 minutes
- Capacity: Maximum 2 people per game.
- Regulations:
+ Adults: From 1m4 can join the game
+ Children: From 1m2-1m4 accompanied by an adult.
+ Under 1m2 are not allowed to participate in the game.
