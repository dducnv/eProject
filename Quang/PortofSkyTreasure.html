Port of Sky Treasure

Inspired by the wild pirate chase on the Malacca (Singapore), this game will hurl you all into non-stop excitements by swaying hard in the air. Type: Extreme. Minimum height: 130cm ( children standing 110cm to 129cm tall must be chaperoned)
Duration: 120 seconds
Number of seats: 20.
Railroad: 680m.
Maximum speed: 80kph
Description: Players are seated aboard and travel on the railroad that features 180-degree twists with a height change of maximum 15.3m that fall free at the speed of 50kph.
